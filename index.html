01 — About

Developer.
Innovator. Builder.

I'm a freelance web developer and ICT student based in Kenya, currently pursuing a Diploma in ICT at the University of Embu. I build functional, modern web applications that solve real problems for real businesses.

My journey into technology was sparked by curiosity — a fascination with how software can change lives. Before turning 20, I had already delivered my first client project: a full-stack web application with M-Pesa payment integration, Kenya's world-famous mobile payment platform.

I believe technology is a bridge between talent and opportunity. My mission is to build platforms and solutions that empower young Kenyans to participate in the global digital economy — regardless of where they come from.
